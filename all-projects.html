SkillMedha CMS Platform

A complex, multi-portal system for students, colleges, and corporate partners.

The Challenge

This massive project required three distinct user portals (Students, Colleges, Corporates). The main challenge was creating a unified Design System that could support complex dashboards for admins while remaining simple enough for students.

My Role & Process

I built a comprehensive component-based Design System in Figma. This included designing role-based access control interfaces and complex data tables. I worked closely with developers to ensure the Figma components translated perfectly to code.

The Outcome

The design system reduced design-to-development handoff time by 30% and ensured a consistent brand identity across all three portals.

TopicPrep Mobile App

A gamified mobile learning app designed to boost student engagement and retention.

The Problem

Students found traditional test prep to be boring and unengaging. The challenge was to create a mobile-first experience that felt like a game but delivered real educational value.

My Role & Process

As the lead UI/UX designer, I was responsible for the entire design process, from user flows and wireframes in Figma to the final high-fidelity mockups. We conducted user interviews and A/B tested two quiz flows, which led to a 25% improvement in usability scores.

The Outcome

The final app design was praised for its intuitive interface and engaging features. The app is currently in the final stages of development and will soon launch on the Google Play Store.
